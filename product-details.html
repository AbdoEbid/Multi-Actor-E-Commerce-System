About the Galaxy S23 Ultra

The Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra is the pinnacle of smartphone technology, combining professional-grade camera capabilities with desktop-level performance. The 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with 120Hz refresh rate delivers stunning visuals, while the new 200MP camera sensor captures incredible detail in any light.

With the built-in S Pen, you get the precision of a pen on your phone, perfect for note-taking, photo editing, or creative work. The Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 for Galaxy processor is optimized specifically for this device, delivering unmatched speed and efficiency.

Designed with premium materials and military-grade durability, the S23 Ultra is as tough as it is beautiful, featuring Gorilla Glass Victus 2 front and back with an armored aluminum frame.
